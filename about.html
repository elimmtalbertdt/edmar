About

Original Pilipino Music (OPM) is a term used to describe music that is composed and performed by Filipino artists. It has a rich history, with roots in traditional Filipino music, and has evolved to include various genres such as pop, rock, folk, and more. OPM reflects the cultural diversity and creativity of the Filipino people.

History

OPM began to take shape in the 1970s with the rise of local bands and solo artists who started to write and perform their own songs in Filipino languages. Over the years, OPM has gained popularity both locally and internationally, showcasing the talent of Filipino musicians.

Why was it called OPM?

The term "OPM" was coined to distinguish music that is original and created by Filipino artists from foreign music. It emphasizes the uniqueness and authenticity of Filipino musical expression.
