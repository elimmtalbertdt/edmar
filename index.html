What is OPM ?

OPM stands for Original Pilipino Music, which refers to music that is composed and performed by Filipino artists. It encompasses a wide range of genres, including pop, rock, folk, and traditional music. OPM has a rich history and has evolved over the years, reflecting the cultural diversity and creativity of the Filipino people.
