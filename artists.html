ARTISTS

TJ Monterde

TJ Monterde is a Filipino singer-songwriter known for his heartfelt ballads and romantic songs. He gained popularity with hits like "Ikaw at Ako" and "Tulad Mo," showcasing his soulful voice and emotional lyrics.

Ben&Ben

Ben&Ben is a folk-pop band from the Philippines, known for their rich harmonies and heartfelt lyrics. Their music often explores themes of love, longing, and social issues, making them a beloved band in the OPM scene.

Cup of Joe

Cup of Joe is a rising OPM band known for their catchy melodies and relatable lyrics. Their music blends various genres, creating a unique sound that resonates with the youth.

Dionela

Dionela is a Filipino singer-songwriter known for his soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics. His music often reflects personal experiences and emotions, making him a standout artist in the OPM scene.

BINI

BINI is a Filipino girl group celebrated for their energetic performances and empowering pop songs. They inspire youth and promote Filipino pride through their music and dynamic stage presence.

SB19

SB19 is a Filipino boy group known for their powerful performances and inspiring songs. They have gained international recognition and promote Filipino talent through their music and dynamic stage presence.
